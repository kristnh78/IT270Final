About Sarah

NameSarah
Age6
Weight2 Tons

Sarah came to rescue as a stray found in Virginia. Her name was given him by the Shelter. She is working on learning it and seems to welcome some encouragement from a simple clap of the hands when calling her. She has been neutered and is UTD on vacinations.

Sarah is housebroken. She will let you know when he needs to go out. She does like to go out in the morning and after every meal.

Sarah knows, "Sit", "Stay" and will offer her paw for a treat. She has a very sweet disposition and loves being near you at all times. She is eager to please and will look to you for praise and confirmation that she is a part of your life.

As noted above, Sarah was found as a stray. Her current foster does not have a fenced yard, and while she is doing quite well with leash walking, her new home will need a fenced yard.
